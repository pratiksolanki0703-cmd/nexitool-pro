Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information NexiTool.pro collects and how it's used. We built the site so that most tools need no personal data at all — read on for the specifics.

Files you process with our tools

Our browser-based tools (image compression, image renaming, PDF compression, and similar utilities) run entirely on your own device using JavaScript in your browser tab. Your files are never uploaded to NexiTool.pro's servers or any third party while using these tools. We have no access to, and do not store, the content of any file you process this way.

Account information (optional)

Creating an account is optional and is only needed if you want to use the coin-based system to access upcoming server-side tools. If you create an account, we (via our backend provider, Supabase) store:

  • Your email address (used for sign-in).
  • Your coin balance and a ledger of coin-earning/spending activity.

We do not sell this information to third parties. It's used only to operate the coin system and your account.

Server-side tools

Some upcoming tools run on our servers rather than in your browser (for example, AI-powered utilities). When you use a server-side tool, the input you submit is sent to our backend and, where applicable, to the third-party AI provider powering that specific tool, solely to generate your result. We do not use this input to train models or share it beyond what's needed to deliver the tool's output.

Cookies and local storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember preferences (like light/dark mode) and to keep you signed in. We may also show ads (including rewarded video ads used to earn coins) served by third-party ad networks, which may use cookies or similar technologies under their own privacy policies.

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which tools are used and improve the site. This does not include the content of any files you process.

Your choices

You can use almost every tool on this site without ever creating an account. If you do create an account, you can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
