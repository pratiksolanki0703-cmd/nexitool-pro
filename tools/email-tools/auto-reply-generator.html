How the Auto-Reply Generator actually works

Paste the incoming email you need to reply to. Optionally add up to two prior messages from the same thread so the AI stays consistent with what's already been said, and optionally tell it what kind of reply you want — decline, confirm, ask for more time, or anything else.

The AI drafts a natural, professional reply (or the tone you asked for) in the output language you select. Every reply you generate is added to your local history so you can find it again later.
