How the Subject Line Generator actually works

Choose "Generate from email body" and paste the body of an email that needs a subject line — the AI returns 5 compelling options, ranked by likely open rate, each kept concise. Choose "Grade a subject line" instead to paste one you've already written for a 0-100 score, a short explanation, and 2-3 improved alternatives.

Every result is added to your local history so you can compare options or come back to a past request later.
