How the AI Email Writer actually works

Choose "Write from scratch" and describe the recipient, the reason for the email, and the single point that matters most — the AI drafts a complete, well-structured email from that context. Choose "Rewrite a draft" instead to paste something rough or unpolished and get it cleaned up while keeping your original meaning intact.

Either way, the AI reads your input regardless of language and always writes the final email in the output language you select — useful for writing comfortably in your first language while sending the result in another.
