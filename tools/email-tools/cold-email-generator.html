How the Cold Email Generator actually works

Describe who you are, who you're reaching out to, and what you're offering or asking for. The AI writes a concise, value-focused cold email that avoids generic outreach filler and gets to the point fast.

Check the follow-up box to also get up to two follow-up emails, each with a distinct angle — a new value point, a gentle nudge, or a final check-in — for when the first email doesn't get a response. Every result is added to your local history so you can find it again later.
