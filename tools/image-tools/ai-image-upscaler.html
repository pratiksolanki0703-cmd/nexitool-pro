Fix pixelated photos and enhance blurry images with AI

This tool runs a lightweight Swin2SR AI super-resolution model directly in your browser using WebAssembly — the same kind of neural network used in research-grade upscalers, just small enough to run on your device. It doubles resolution (2x) while reconstructing detail that a simple resize would blur.

Because it's a real model rather than a stretch-and-sharpen filter, results on pixelated or low-resolution source images tend to look noticeably cleaner than a plain resize.
