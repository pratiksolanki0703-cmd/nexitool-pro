A TinyPNG alternative that never uploads your photos

Set a target size like 50KB and this tool searches for the highest quality that still lands close to it — or skip the target and use the quality slider directly. Because compression runs on-device, there's no file size limit imposed by a server, and no copy of your photos sitting anywhere else afterward.

Works well for trimming a camera roll before an upload, or getting product photos under a platform's size cap without a noticeable quality drop.

How it works

  • Drop in one photo or a whole batch — no size limit

  • Optionally switch the output format
  • Click Compress All and watch progress update live

Why bother shrinking photos?

Pages load faster when the images on them are lighter.
Uploads stop bouncing off size limits on forms and platforms.
Storage stretches further once duplicates aren't both full-size.
Sharing gets quicker over slow or metered connections.
