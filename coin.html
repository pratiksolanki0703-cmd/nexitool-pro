What is Coin?

Last updated: July 2026

Coin is NexiTool.pro's free reward system. You earn coins without paying any money, then spend them on advanced server-side tools (like AI features). All browser-based tools stay free forever — coins are purely optional, for users who want access to more powerful tools.

How do I earn coins?

Create a free account and earn coins in two ways:

  • Passively: Just by keeping NexiTool.pro open in your browser, you earn 5 coins every 30 seconds. No action needed. Your account will accrue coins automatically while the site is active.
  • Watch ads: Click the "Watch Ad" button and complete a short rewarded video. You'll earn 30 bonus coins in addition to your passive earning.

Key point: Earning coins is 100% free. You never pay money, and coins are not for sale. They are earned only.

What do I use coins for?

Right now, all tools on NexiTool.pro are free (browser-based). But we're building advanced server-side tools that require coins:

  • AI text-to-image: Generate images from text descriptions.
  • AI text-to-speech: Convert text to natural-sounding voice.
  • AI chat & coding: Chat with AI or generate code snippets.
  • Advanced video tools: Edit or enhance videos using AI.

These tools will "cost" coins to use, but you earn those coins for free (as described above). So you can use them without ever paying.

What's the catch? Is there a payment option?

No catch. There is no payment option on NexiTool.pro — you cannot buy coins with real money. You earn coins only. This keeps the site 100% free to use, funded by the ads you optionally watch.

What if I don't watch ads?

You don't have to watch ads. You'll still earn coins passively (5 coins every 30 seconds). Watching ads is optional and gives you bonus coins if you want them.

Is there a limit to how many coins I can earn?

Yes, there's a safety cap: maximum 90 coins per minute from all earning combined (passive + ads). This prevents abuse. In practice, passive earning alone (5 coins/30 seconds = 10 coins/minute) is well under this cap, so you'll always be able to earn at a steady rate.

What if I leave the browser or close the tab?

Passive earning stops. The coin-earning loop requires an active browser tab so we can detect genuine usage. Close the tab, and your passive earn rate stops immediately. Open it again, and you start earning again.

Can I transfer coins to another account or person?

No. Coins are tied to your account only and cannot be transferred, shared, or traded. They're for your personal use only.

Will coins ever expire?

Coins don't expire. They stay in your account indefinitely. Use them whenever you want.

Example: How much does a server-side tool cost in coins?

We haven't launched server-side tools yet, so prices aren't final. But here's an example:

  • Generating one AI image might cost 50 coins.
  • At passive earning (5 coins/30 sec), you'd earn 50 coins in about 5 minutes of having the site open.
  • Or you could watch one rewarded ad (30 coins) + passive earn for 2 minutes (10 coins) to reach 50 coins in minutes.

Is my account and coins safe?

Yes. Coins are stored server-side (in Supabase) and tied to your verified email account. They're not stored locally in your browser, so they can't be tampered with. Your balance is always verified on the server before any tool uses your coins.
