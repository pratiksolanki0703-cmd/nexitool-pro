About NexiTool.pro

Last updated: July 2026

NexiTool.pro is a small, growing kit of single-purpose online tools. The goal is simple: give people a fast way to do one job — compress a batch of photos, rename files, shrink a PDF — without installing an app or fighting through ads and pop-ups to find a download button.

How the free tools work

Most tools on NexiTool.pro (image compression, image renaming, PDF compression, and more as they're added) run entirely inside your browser tab using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to a server — everything happens on your own device, and nothing you process is seen or stored by us.

Why there's an optional account

NexiTool.pro is introducing a small set of server-side tools (like AI-powered utilities) that cost real backend resources to run. To keep those tools free for users without charging real money, we're building a coin system: you can optionally create a free account and earn coins by watching ads, then spend those coins on server-side tools. The browser-based tools described above will always stay free and will never require an account.
